Polyurethane resins offer a versatile range of material properties, including Medium Firm (MF) formulations with moderate hardness and flexibility for coatings, seals, and molded parts, as well as Rigid Tough (RT) formulations with high stiffness, toughness, and impact resistance for structural parts, tooling, and industrial components.

Medium Firm (MF) polyurethane resins typically have a moderate hardness or stiffness in the range of 4mm to 6mm on a Shore hardness scale, and offer a balance between flexibility and rigidity.
